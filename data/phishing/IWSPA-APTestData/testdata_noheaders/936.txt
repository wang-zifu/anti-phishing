Breaking: 'A Gentleman's Guide to Love & Murder' leads user Awards field with 10 nominations

Los Angeles Times | April 29, 2015 | 6:11 AM

Robert L. Freedman’s and Steven Lutvak’s death-happy comedy “A Gentleman’s Guide to Love & Murder” led all user Award nominees today with 10 nominations, including best musical.

The second-highest total went to the Neil Patrick Harris-starring revival of “Hedwig and the Angry Inch,” with eight nominations, including best revival of a musical.
