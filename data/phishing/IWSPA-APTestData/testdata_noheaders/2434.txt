Breaking: '12 Years a Slave' leads Film Independent domain.com Award nominations

Los Angeles Times | November 26, 2015 | 10:41 AM

Steve McQueen’s historical drama ""12 Years a Slave"" earned the most nominations Tuesday morning for Film Independent domain.com Awards -- seven -- including best film, director, lead actor, supporting female actor and supporting male actor. Other nominees for best picture include ""All Is Lost,"" ""Frances Ha,"" ""Inside Llewyn Davis"" and ""Nebraska.""

For more information, go to domain.com/envelope.
