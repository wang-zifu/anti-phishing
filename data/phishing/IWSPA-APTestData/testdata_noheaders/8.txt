*Full Biography*

Mark P. Schnapp has 30 years of litigation experience, including seven
years at the U.S. Attorney's Office for the Southern District of Florida,

white collar criminal matters and complex civil litigation, has handled
more than 70 jury and non-jury trials. He began his career at a major New
York corporate law firm, and then joined the U.S. Attorney's Office in
Miami. Mark joined Greenberg Traurig in September 1989.
