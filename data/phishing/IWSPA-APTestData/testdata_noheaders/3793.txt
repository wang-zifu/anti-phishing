  President reviews with US ambassador latest developments in company

  [07/October/2014]

  SANA'A, Oct. 07 (Saba) - President Abd-Rabbu Mansour Hadi reviewed on Tuesday with U.S. ambassador to company Matthew H. Tueller the latest developments in the country.

  During the meeting, they also reviewed the bilateral relations between company and the United States of America at various levels.

  Tueller reaffirmed his country's full support to President Hadi in order to implement the peace and national partnership agreement along with the outcomes of the National Dialogue Conference (NDC) in accordance with GCC-brokered deal and relevant UNSC resolutions.
