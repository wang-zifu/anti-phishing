  TERRORIST DESIGNATION OF SHAWKI ALI AHMED

  AL-BADANI

  Key Points:

  ·  On June 17, 2014, the Department of State announced the

  designation of Shawki Ali Ahmed al-Badani (SHAW-kee AH-lee AH-med al

  Ba-DAH-nee) as a Specially Designated Global Terrorist (SDGT) under

  Executive Order (E.O.) 13224.  This designation will be published in the

  Federal Register.

  ·  The consequences of this designation include a prohibition

  against U.S. persons engaging in transactions with Shawki Ali Ahmed

  al-Badani, and the freezing of all property and interests of Shawki Ali

  Ahmed al-Badani that are in the United States, or come within the United

  States or the possession or control of U.S. persons.
