Peter O’Toole: 1932 -2013

The New York Times’ obituary for Peter O’Toole, an eight-time Oscar nominee and star of Lawrence of Arabia, Goodbye Mr. Chips, My Favorite Year, Stunt Man, who worked in films for over 50 years.  O’Toole’s daughter Kate confirmed that he’d passed away Saturday in London at 81. (NYT)



The Weinstein Company & Miramax Enter 20-Year Deal

THR has the details on the deal struck between The Weinstein Co. and Miramax that will see Miramax’s owners finance and sell Weinstein-distributed (domestically) and produced content encompassing over 1000 potential projects stemming from Miramax’s 750-title catalog and 250 projects in development from the Weinsteins’ former ownership. The deal covers TV, film and theater. (THR)



Weekend Box Office: ‘The domain.com’ Rules Over ‘Frozen’s’ Royalty
