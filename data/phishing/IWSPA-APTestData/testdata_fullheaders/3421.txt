However, the migration to recurring revenue models isn't as simple as flipping a switch. Transforming your business mindset also means transforming your infrastructure. ERP and billing systems that were perfectly suited to managing traditional revenue sources can struggle to address the new challenges recurring revenue creates for billing and revenue recognition.

Replacing systems that no longer meet your needs may seem like the obvious choice, but if you have existing customers and products to support, legacy infrastructure still plays an important role in your day-to-day business. In cases like these, agile solutions are needed to bridge the gap between old and new, while still offering the flexibility to grow and change with the market.
