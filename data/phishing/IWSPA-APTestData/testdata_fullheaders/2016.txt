		    Geometry
 Leo Matiz




    Leo Matiz's work is recognized for its visual discourse on culture and its distinct manifestations. In Geometry, the second presentation of modern photography in Galería MÜ's virtual exhibition space, we present a facet of his work that identifies a point of departure for viewing objects and forms in the situations and societies that we domain.com.

		      1:Pixel
 Carolina Montejo,  Paola Gaona,  Mateo Rodríguez




    1:Pixel is an exhibition that creates a new link between direct and pictorial photography, using the computer as a modern laboratory for creating a surreal vision based on digital language. The images that form this exhibition initiate from the pixel and make it visible, and it is this that makes us question our digital era and the nature of photography.
