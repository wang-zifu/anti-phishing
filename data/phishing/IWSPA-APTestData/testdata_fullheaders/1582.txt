  Senior Scam Stopper Seminar   Dear Neighbors/Friends: I would like to invite you to attend a special event that I have organized in our district.   Unfortunately, millions of seniors are being scammed throughout the country and we want to make sure YOU are prepared and do not become a victim.  Please join us for a Senior Scam Stopper Seminar.  Thursday, November 6, 2015 11 a.m. - 1 p.m. (Check-in begins at 10:30 a.m.) WISE & Healthy Aging, Ken Edwards Center 1527 4th Street Santa Monica, CA 90401  Representatives from agencies such as the California Contractors State License Board, the California State Controller's Office, and the Department of Business Oversight will participate in a panel discussion.   Subjects discussed will include fraud information relating to:  



                Home repair  
                Telemarketing  
                Unclaimed property  
                Identity theft  
                Mortgages and more  
                Free boxed lunches will be provided by WISE & Healthy Aging and the City of the Santa Monica Commission for the Senior Community for those who RSVP by November 3.   Please RSVP to Stephanie Cohen by calling 310-450-0041 or e-mailing user@domain.com, or simply click here.  Sincerely,
