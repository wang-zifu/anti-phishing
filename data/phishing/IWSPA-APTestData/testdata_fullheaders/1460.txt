Reply to this email to RSVP if you are interested in attending the meeting.

GAC's Outdoor Leadership Course (OLC) offers two weeks of fun, friendship, and growth for older campers (9th-11th grade) who are ready for a leadership and wilderness adventure!

 <<link>>

Just a few highlights of the program...

Designed to train and prepare young people to be future leaders who influence self, others, and culture through experiences in the wilderness.

Earn Wilderness First Aid certification.

Learn and practice backcountry navigation and safety, outdoor cooking, "Leave No Trace" principles and ethics, and wildlife biology.

Led by five-year GAC veteran backpacking instructor, Jonathan "Mufasa" Bower.
