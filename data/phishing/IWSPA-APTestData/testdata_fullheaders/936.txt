Trendsetter Business Outlook - Webcast: Oct 22




			Dear Amy,



			You are cordially invited to attend a webcast panel discussion entitled "Trendsetter Business Outlook - a conversation about economic trends impacting private companies and C-suite decisions." The webcast will be held on October 22 from 1:30 - 3:00pm eastern.



			The panel discussion will focus on business decisions that private companies should consider in light of key economic indicators revealed in the quarterly Trendsetter Barometer report. The panel will include Ken Esch, Private Company Services partner who oversees the PCS Trendsetter program, Fentress Seagroves, PwC Transaction Services partner focused on M&A, David Ekizian, General Manager of Mitsui & Co (USA) and Jeff Prosinski, Vice President and Chief Financial Officer for J.M. Huber Corporation.



			The discussion will be moderated by Ron Insana, senior analyst and commentator at CNBC.
			Topics will include: 




				What’s the outlook for growth and revenues in the U.S. and abroad?
				Should growth strategies be recalculated to reflect new realities?
				Is now the time to make major capital investments?
				What impact will M&A activity have on company plans?
				What are companies doing to drive topline sales growth?
				What are companies doing to add or retain qualified talent as labor markets improve?



			Who should attend? Private company executives and decision makers, and family business owners.

			About Private Company Services’ Trendsetter Business Outlook
			Trendsetter Barometer is a quarter business report that takes the pulse of more than 200 private company CEOs on the economy, performance expectations, and other key business issues. The findings show trends in attitudes and how these executives look at hiring, international expansion, barriers and opportunities for growth in light of key economic indicators.
