	Dear Amy,

	Each year, our Federation makes it possible for thousands of young adults to experience our Jewish homeland for free through Birthright Israel. Hundreds more will create a lifelong connection to Israel this winter, because registration for our Federation’s Birthright Israel L.A. Way Winter 2015 trips is now open!

	Birthright Israel L.A. Way trips fly young adults between the ages of 18-26 from L.A. to Tel Aviv with other Angelenos for a 10-day trip. Together, they discover the country’s rich history, landmarks, people and culture while exploring their Jewish identities. Once they return home, our Federation keeps participants connected to one another, to Israel and to our Jewish community. This is just one of the impactful ways we are ensuring the Jewish future—with your support!

	This winter, L.A. Way participants can choose one of several adventures with niche trip options for foodies, grad students and entertainment professionals—our entertainment bus is even accepting young adults up to age 29!

	Learn more about our Birthright Israel L.A. Way Winter 2015 trips!

	Space is limited, so if you or someone you know is 18-26 years old (or 29 for entertainment professionals), please share this e-mail and encourage them to register for one of our life-changing Birthright Israel L.A. Way experiences today.
